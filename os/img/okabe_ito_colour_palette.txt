These colours toghether form the Okabe-ito colour palette.
This palette is a “Set of colors that is unambiguous both to colorblinds and non-colorblinds”.
The use of this palette is supported by others (Wong, 2011; Levine, 2009) and it is the default scale for the book “Fundamentals of Data Visualization” by Claus Wilke.
